Centurion Janitorial Services: Your Cleaning Solution

Centurion Services was founded in 2006 in San Francisco, California. Currently based in the Peninsula, Centurion serves a variety of clients throughout the Bay Area. The clients we serve include commercial buildings such as hospitals, clinics, schools, office buildings, fitness centers, manufacturing facilities among others. Distinguished by a willingness to provide a highly personalized service and basic hard work, Centurion provides its clients with individualized and customized services to meet any maintenance, janitorial and handyman service. Consistent communication with Centurion’s clientele ensures that our technicians are always conforming to the client’s needs and high standards. Certifications: Centurion Services follows all of OSHA cleaning standards and provides our janitorial crew members with strong training in safety.

  • EPA Renovator Certification
  • Environmental Consulting and Training
  • Green Business Bureau Certified
